HCM AI Challenge 2021: My Journey to Top 4

December 30, 2021 Competition 5 min read

Overview

The HCM AI Challenge 2021 was a significant event aimed at promoting the application of artificial intelligence in daily life. The competition focused on Vietnamese Scene Text Recognition, attracting over 330 teams with more than 1,000 participants from Vietnam and Southeast Asian countries.

Competition Details

Duration:

October 2021 - December 30, 2021

Challenge:

Vietnamese Scene Text Recognition in natural images and daily life scenarios

Participants:

330+ teams, 1,000+ contestants

Organization

The competition was organized by:

  • Ho Chi Minh City Department of Information and Communications
  • Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City
  • Department of Science and Technology
  • Department of Education and Training
  • Ho Chi Minh City Computer Association
  • Youth Science and Technology Development Center
  • Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union

Technical Challenge

The competition was structured similarly to international scientific competitions, seeking effective solutions for emerging problems in Vietnamese text recognition. Participants were challenged to develop AI models capable of accurately recognizing Vietnamese text in various real-world scenarios.

Our Approach

As part of the competition, our team focused on developing robust solutions for Vietnamese text recognition in natural scenes. We implemented various deep learning techniques and addressed challenges specific to Vietnamese language characteristics.

Key Technical Aspects:

  • Advanced OCR techniques
  • Deep learning models optimization
  • Vietnamese language-specific processing
  • Real-time recognition capabilities

Results and Achievement

Our team successfully secured a position in the Top 4, competing against 330+ teams. This achievement not only validated our technical approach but also provided valuable opportunities for future development.

Benefits and Opportunities:

  • Access to specialized training sessions
  • Networking with industry experts and scientists
  • Support for solution refinement
  • Connections with e-government implementation units

Impact and Future Prospects

The competition has played a crucial role in promoting AI research and applications in Ho Chi Minh City and Vietnam. It has created opportunities for young talents to contribute to the city's smart city development initiatives and encouraged innovative solutions for daily life challenges.
